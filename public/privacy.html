Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect resume content you submit for analysis, your email address if provided for delivery of rewritten resumes, and basic usage analytics (page views, no personal data).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your resume content is used solely to generate AI feedback and rewritten versions. We do not store resume content after processing is complete. Email addresses are used only to deliver your rewritten resume.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Resume content is processed via the Anthropic Claude API under their privacy policy. Payments are handled by Paddle under their privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

Resume content is not retained after processing. Email addresses are retained only until service delivery is complete.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for basic site functionality only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any data we hold about you. Contact us at support@roastcv.app to make such a request.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at support@roastcv.app
